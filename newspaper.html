Guntur: Price of premium red chilli varieties likely to rise

GUNTUR🌶:

 Premium varieties of red chilli prices are likely to touch Rs 24,000 per quintal during this season,thanks to the unprecedented increase in the demand for red chillies in the domestic market.

 According to red chilli traders at the Guntur Mirchi Yard, at present 341 variety of red chilli is being sold at Rs 21,500 per quintal. Price of this variety likely to touch Rs 24,000 per quintal.
